FAQs

How much does joining cost?
Dues are around $200 each semester
When are practices?
We typically practice Monday & Wednesday on Vert, 6:30-8:00 pm.
When is the season?
We play both in the fall & spring.
The fall is typically less of a committment as it is not for rankings.
How many games are there?
In Fall 2023 we played around 4 games, in Spring 2024 we played around 8 games, not including regionals.
Do you travel?
We typically travel around North Carolina to schools like App State, UNC Wilmington, UNC Charlotte, ECU, & USC.
For regionals in 2023 we traveled to Pennsylvania and in 2024 we will travel to Maryland to play in regionals again.
